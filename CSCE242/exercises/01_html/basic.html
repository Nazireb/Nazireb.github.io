Kevin Durant

By Nazire Brown

Kevin Durant is a American professional basketball player that plays in the NBA. He was born in Washington D.C. and went to the University of Texas. Kevin then went on to get drafted in 2007 to the Seattle Supersonics. He currently is playing for the Phoenix Suns but has played on other teams through out his career. Those teams include the Seattle Supersonics(Are now the Oklahoma City Thunder), Golden State Warriors and the Brooklyn Nets.
