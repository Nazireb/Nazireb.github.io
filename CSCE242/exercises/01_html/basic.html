Kevin Durant

Info about Kevin Durant


NBA top 75 (he is number 12)

Kevin Durant is a American professional basketball player that plays in the NBA. He was born in Washington D.C. and went to the University of Texas. Kevin then went on to get drafted in 2007 to the Seattle Supersonics. He currently is playing for the Phoenix Suns but has played on other teams through out his career. Those teams include the Seattle Supersonics (Are now the Oklahoma City Thunder), Golden State Warriors and the Brooklyn Nets.

Since coming into the NBA, Kevin Durant has seen a lot of success. He has played a total of 16 seasons in the NBA and has been an All-Star 14 times, has 2 NBA championshis along with 2 finals MVP, a regualr season MVP and many more accolades. To expand on his success he is ranked number 12 on the NBA top 75 list (Which is a list of the best players to ever play in the NBA). Kevin Durant is overall a great basketball player and has cemented his name into the history of the NBA.

Facts about him

  • Is the most decorated men's basketball player in olympic mens basketball history with 4 gold medals.
  • Is the only player with MVPs in the World cup, NBA and the Olympics
  • Is 1 of 3 people who have a lifetime deal with Nike
